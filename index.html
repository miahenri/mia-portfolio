ABOUT ME

MIA HENRICHSMEYER

Hi, I’m Mia, originally from Bielefeld, where I graduated from Ratsgymnasium in 2021. Creativity has always been a big part of who I am, and in high school, I discovered my love for technology through the STEM subjects. It was in 10th grade that I really realized my talent for computer science.

After graduating, I spent a year working different jobs and doing internships to explore the field that would be the right fit for me. In 2022, I moved to Cologne to study Media Informatics at TH Köln. During my studies, I gained a lot of practical experience in areas like frontend and backend development, audiovisual media, and human-computer interaction.

Now I’m excited to take on new projects and challenges. If you’re looking for creative and modern solutions, feel free to reach out—I’d love to hear from you!
